TouchNot_App

PROJECT APP IDEA: TouchNot App
DESCRIPTION: The "TouchNot" App keeps record of the information of anyone that uses your laptop. It has the ability to pick up finger-prints on the "Touch-pad", "Mouse" and on the "Enter key '`keyCode ==13'`".
The app tells;
1. Who used your laptop via finger-print identification.
2.  When (date/time) they used your laptop.
3. Gives you the details of what they did with your system.
